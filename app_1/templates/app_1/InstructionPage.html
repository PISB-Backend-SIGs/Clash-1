instructions

  1. Welcome to Clash Round 1 of Credenz 2023 !

  2. The quiz consists of multiple choice questions each having a maximum one correct choice. Please make sure to read the questions carefully and mark the appropriate choice.

  3. The marking scheme varies according to your gameplay which would be reflected on the screen.Marking scheme is as follows:
    1. +4 -2(If previous question is correct)
    2. +2 -1(If previous question is wrong)
    3. -1(If question not attempted)
    4. The marking scheme wil vary for lifelines. (Its explained in individual lifeline)

  4. To help you with the quiz we have provided 3 lifelines.
    1. Black Hole
    2. Back to the Future
    3. Ask TARS

  5. The duration of the quiz is 28 minutes. On completion of time,the quiz will be automatically submitted. Please make sure that you don't switch the tab in which the quiz is going on.On 3 times tab switching your test will be automatically submitted.

  6. Happy Coding!

lifeline 1

  1. Black Hole


  2. Are you unsure about option A or B? Don't worry, this lifeline will erase the two wrong options and increase your probability of getting the correct answer.

    But the Twist is - You must have a streak of 3 or more correct answers to use this up!

  3. Note that your streak will reset once you use this lifeline.
    The marking scheme on using this lifeline will be -5 if you get the question wrong.


lifeline 2

  1. Back to the Future

  2. Oh no! You made a mistake in the last question . Use this lifeline to rewind the time and go back to the previous question and get a chance to correct it .

    Precondition is that you have total score of least 7 to use this up and accuracy must be greater than or equal to 50%.

    The marking scheme on using this lifeline will be -4 if you get the answer wrong.




lifeline 3

  1. Ask TARS

  2. AI is taking all over the world, then why not use it in coding contests? We bring to you TARS Assistance, an AI based assistance that answers your every question and helps you out when you need it .

    This assistance will assist you only once and only if you solve more than 5 questions .

    There is no change in the marking shceme for this lifeline.

  3. Please check the checkbox and proceed upon reading the instructions.
